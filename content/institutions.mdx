
## For Institutions  

Culturally responsive language development through story, culture, and immersion.

### The Challenge

Many institutions are working to support multilingual learners—but:  

* Engagement is inconsistent  
* Heritage language is often lost over time  
* Instruction lacks cultural depth and connection

### The Creolitos™ Approach

Creolitos™ delivers story-based, culturally grounded programming that supports language development while affirming identity.

Our work is rooted in the **Fiesta Methodology™**, which integrates:

* Storytelling as the foundation for language  
* Performance and movement for deeper engagement  
* Cultural context to make language meaningful and relevant

### Outcomes

* Increased engagement in language learning  
* Stronger comprehension through context and repetition  
* Reinforced cultural identity and belonging

### Program Formats

- **In-School Residencies**  

   Short-term immersive experiences inspired by the Summer Reading Fiesta™ model  
- **After-School Programs**  

   Ongoing enrichment for language exposure and development  
- **Family Engagement Events**  
   Interactive bilingual experiences that bring families into the learning process  
- **Professional Development**  
   Training for educators on story-based, culturally responsive language practices

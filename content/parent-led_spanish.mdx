A guided experience where caregivers become the driver of their child’s language development.
Using curated board books, families learn Spanish through story, supported by:

- Simple, practical language and grammar explained in context
- Songs and repetition strategies
- ASL to reinforce vocabulary
- Take-home materials (printable visuals, recordings of songs and readings)

_The goal is not just exposure; it’s helping parents confidently bring Spanish into daily life._

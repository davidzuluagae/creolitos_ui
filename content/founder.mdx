María is a Colorado Licensed Teacher and native Spanish speaker with over 8 years of experience teaching multilingual learners in K–8 classrooms. She holds:
  - A Bachelor’s in Philosophy & Education
  - A Master’s in Children’s Literature & Reading

Through her work, María identified two critical gaps:

Families. especially immigrant and second and third-generation households, wanted to preserve their language and heritage, but lacked the tools and support to do it.
At the same time, schools and institutions were serving multilingual learners without culturally grounded approaches that truly engage students or sustain language development.
Creolitos™ was created to bridge both gaps—through story-driven, immersive experiences that connect language, culture, and identity in lasting ways.
